SFO Ascent Logo

The Ascent logo has been designed to delineate large construction projects that are ongoing at the airport. It is designed in conjunction with a series of graphics and images that tell the story of several signature projects, including the Air Traffic Control Tower, Terminal 3 Boarding Area E (and affiliated projects) and other upcoming projects at the airport.

The Consumer Communications logo is used for:

• Website
• Wayfinding Graphics
• Airport Signage
• Uniforms
• Advertising
• Social Media
• Promotions
• Communications

As such, we have developed guidelines and standards for its consistent usage across a variety of situations and media. In general, the preferred version for use in the majority of situations is the color version on a white background. In cases where the logo must appear on a dark background the secondary reversed logo should be used.
