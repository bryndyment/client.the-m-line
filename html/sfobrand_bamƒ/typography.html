Typography

Primary Typeface

Myriad is available in a variety of weights and styles. It is a highly legible contemporary sans serif typeface that is ideal for headlines, large display type and corporate applications such as business cards and stationery.

Secondary Typeface

Rotis Serif, which works well with Myriad, is available in limited weights and styles. Rotis is used for text in SFO brochures and the "Update" newsletter.
