Logo

The MeasureOne logo has been prepared for use in a variety of situations and media. Digital files for all versions are available on the downloads page.

The preferred version for use in the majority of situations is the color version on a white background. In cases where the logo must appear on a dark background the secondary reversed color logo should be used.

Printing Restrictions

Color printing may not always be available or appropriate for all printed communications. If only black ink is available, use shades of black (grayscale) to reproduce the logo. Do not use this version with any color other than black.
