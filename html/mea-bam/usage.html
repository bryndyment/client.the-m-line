Logo Usage

Clear Space

Maintaining an area around the logos, known as clear space, will ensure the logos stand out from the other elements such as text, photography and other logos. Determine the clear space by measuring the height of the M character in the MeasureOne wordmark element and then applying this distance to all sides of the logo.

Minimum Size

To avoid compromising the legibility and overall quality of the MeasureOne logo, adhere to the minimum size requirements. When resizing the logo, ensure that both the logomark and the wordmark are resized together so their proportion to each other remains constant.
