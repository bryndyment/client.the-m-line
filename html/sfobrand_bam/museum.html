SFO Museum Logo

SFO Museum's logo is to be used on all communications coming from SFO Museum, and is to be used at their discretion. Usage of this logo should be cleared with SFO Museum in advance.

We have developed guidelines and standards for its consistent usage across a variety of situations and media. In general, the preferred version for use in the majority of situations is the color version on a white background. In cases where the logo must appear on a dark background the secondary reversed logo should be used.
